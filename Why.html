Why

Logical fallacies are used for all kinds of reasons. Most times we are unaware that we are guilty of fallacious reasoing. And other times.. We know we are guilty, but refuse to acknowledge the fallacy and continue on in our reasoning. I created this page to help identify common fallacies and raise awareness of the not so common ones as well.
